    Fried gnocchi with peas and pesto

    Ingredients:

    • Gnocchi
    • Peas
    • Pesto
    • Side of steamed greens

    Method:

    1. Boil the gnocchi until they float in the water.
    2. Heat a frying pan or wok with oil, and add the gnocchi.
    3. Meanwhile, heat some peas in the microwave, and steam some greens in the microwave.
    4. When the gnochhi is browned, add the peas and pesto to the pan to mox and warm through.
    5. Serve gnocchi with steamed greens on the side.

    Side: Steamed greens

    Ingredients:

    • Any leafy greens

    Method:

    1. Rinse the leave and chop them into strips.
    2. Put them in a microwaveable container with a very small splash of water (1tsp) and microwave for 1 minute.
    3. Before serving, add salt, pepper, and olive oil.
